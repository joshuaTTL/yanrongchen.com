PINEAPPLE PIE

currently available in appstore

Project Objective:
Pineapple pie is created with the goal of providing eating disorder recovery support (especially to those with Binge Eating Disorder or Bulimia Nervosa).

Project Background: I initiated this project after researching and learning about the conditions of people who experience Binge Eating Disorder and Bulimia Nervosa. I wished to create something that would accompany them and help them on their recovery journey.

In order to “make it possible”, I teamed up with Situo Meng, the developer of the product, and we launched Pineapple Pie on appstore in September, 2020.

Those who struggle with the disorder will typically:

- eat a large amount of food rapidly, despite not feeling hungry
- tend to eat alone or in secret
- feel ashamed, guilty, or a lack of control about eating
- compenste by fasting, exercising excessively, or purging (Bulimia)
- have unhealthy stress over body image and weight

↓

Design solutions of Pineapple Pie:

Intuitive eating

To trust your body, and to eat when you’re hungry and stop when you’re full.

It is opposite of a traditional diet; it doesn’t impose guidelines about what to eat or when to eat. Instead, it encourages you to honor your hunger, and honor your feelings without using food.

“Pineapple Pie” encourages people to take a moment from the obsession of “dieting” and “losing weight”, and take their time to restore their fullness cues, redefine what eating “really means”, and develop a healthier relationship with food.

At the end of the day, I made this app with the goal of helping people alleviate suffering and overcome their struggles with weight and image, and eventually build higher self-esteem and self-worth.
