01

The challenge

 

People tend to become more forgetful as they age. Many senior citizens (especially those with Alzheimer's) do not just occasionally forget where they left their personal belonging - their reading glasses, keys, wallets, pills and many more; it can be very frustrating when they are frequently unable to locate the “missing” items.

There are some personal item tracking devices in the market now. However, none of those are designed specifically for older generations.
