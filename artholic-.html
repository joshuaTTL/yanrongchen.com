PERSONAL PROJECT | 3 WEEKS | MOBILE APP DESIGN

 

Project summary:

ARTHOLIC is a mobile app dedicated to:

  1. Inject art into users’ everyday life

  2. Help private art galleries and museums to thrive by increasing attendance and making additional profits


 

TOOL:

Figma | Sketch | Adobe XD | Photoshop | Illustrator | Protopie

 
 
 


Background Research

  1. Global Private Art Museums Landscape

    On a global scale, privately founded art museums have become an indispensable platform for art shows and art education. However, there are sources and evidence suggesting that people (specifically, ages 18-65) are becoming less inclined to visit museums and galleries in recent years.

2. Case Study - Private Art Museum Market in China

I analyzed the operation expense for running a private museum per year and their sources of revenue.

 

Other Findings:

  1. Those who would see marketing through museum’s homepage or social media are usually already interested in the visiting the museum/gallery. General population do not know what is currently exhibiting, or they do not know how it could be personally relevant to them.

  2. Other barriers preventing people from visiting: entry fees, unconventional opening hours, previous negative experience, lack of company, and etc.

USABILITY TEST

 

Key Changes After Usability Test

  1. Remove Daily Art Quiz, Star/Calendar/Daily Sign-in Features - Users don’t want to take any form of quiz; daily sign in and stars are confusing.

  2. Change Front Page Design - Some users liked the appropriations of the artworks, others felt that appropriations were not what they were looking for; I changed the front page design to focus more on providing an easier access to the exhibitions nearby.
